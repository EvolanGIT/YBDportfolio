About Me:

Hello! My name is Yonatan Bermudez. This website is designed to showcase my work while I learn about web design. This will also display links to all my work while I progress and become more proficient in this line of work. I am fluent in Spanish and English and enjoy playing Rugby. I'm also an Anime and gaming fan who would like to design apps and mobile games as well as websites for all kind of content for the gaming industry. I am part of a small group of friends who all decided to learn together and we call ourselves "The four codesmen".
